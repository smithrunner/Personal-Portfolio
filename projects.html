Colorado School of Mines (August 2012- May 2016): Geophysical Engineering

    Geophysical Engineering is a natural science concerned with the physical processes and properties of the Earth, analyized through quantitative methods. To put it concisely, a geophycists is a combination of a computer scientist, geologist and physicist.

Bloc (June 2017 - Current): Software Development Program

    Bloc is an online coding bootcamp lasting 8 months when working at the full time pace of 40 hours per week. The program has an emphasis on giving students real world coding experience through independent projects and mentorship. The program teaches Javascript, AngularJS, Ruby and Ruby-on-Rails.
