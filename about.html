I am currently living in Austin, Texas which is about 3 hours south of my home town of Southlake. That might seem like a pretty straight forward life story but I took the most roundabout way to move those 3 hours. After graduating high school in 2012, I moved to Golden, Colorado to complete my B.S. in Geophysical Engineering from the Colorado School of Mines. Once I graduated in 2016 I realized snow sucks so I moved to San Luis Obispo, CA where I worked for the tech company MINDBODY in various technical support roles for a year. My time in California was full of amazing adventures, beaches, friends and a personal journey to find a more fullfilling career. On a whim I decided to buy a van and live in it. The following 4 months were pure hell for my worried mother in Texas but the most fun time of my life. I traveled all along the central coast of California while applying for different jobs, schools and educational programs. Eventually I decided to pursue Software Development through the online bootcamp Bloc.io while living in Austin, Texas. In June of 2017, I hopped in the van once again to drive home to Texas to pursue a career in software development.

I started racing triathlon at the age of 9 and my love for the sport has only grown over the last 14+ years. I ran cross country and track through highschool and then once in college, made triathlon my main focus. I was fortunate enough to be president of the Mines Triathlon Team and travel with them for races all over the country. College racing is sprint and olympic distance which was a blast but I believe I am better suited for half-iron and ironman distance racing. A tibia stress fracture in October of 2016 put a pause on my triathlon training for awhile but as of July 2017 I am back training and racing!
